Hush, sun, hush is a massively multiplayer rhythm game. Work together with other worshippers around the world to sooth the sun to sleep.
 
Make a shushing pattern, and then repeat it over and over to sooth the baby. It works even better if you match rhythms with the other players. Play with headphones and zone out to the soothing sounds of the wind and waves.
Made for Molyjam 2012.
Based on a tweet of Peter Molydeux.
